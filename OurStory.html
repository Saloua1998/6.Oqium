OUR STORY

Founded by sneaker collectors, Oqium wanted to give the community something different that would give them a new vision by infusing the world of basketball into the sneakergame. Unlocking the game on and off court, making everyone part of it.

Oqium first opened its doors in 2008, in the centre of Rotterdam. As one of the only stores with a wide range of basketball gear and exclusive sneakers, Oqium connected the basketball scene and sneaker culture.

With 2 stores based in Amsterdam and Rotterdam, Oqium offers a wide range of performance basketball products, NBA gear and lifestyle sneakers and apparel.

Oqium, against all odds.
